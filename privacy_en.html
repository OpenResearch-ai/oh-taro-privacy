1. Overview

Oh Taro ("we", "our", or "the app") is an AI-powered tarot reading and counseling app developed by OpenResearch AI. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Email address, phone number, or real name (unless voluntarily shared in chat)
  • Location data
  • Contacts or photos
  • Browsing history

4. How We Use Your Information

  • Providing AI-powered tarot readings and conversation
  • Sending re-engagement push notifications
  • Improving service quality through anonymized analytics

6. Data Retention

Your data is retained until you delete the app or request deletion. You can reset your session at any time via Settings → New Conversation.

7. Your Rights

  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out: Disable push notifications via device settings

8. Children's Privacy

This app is intended for users aged 17 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
